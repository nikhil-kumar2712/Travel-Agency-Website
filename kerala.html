Day 01 : Arrive Cochin – Munnar

On Arrival you will be received from Cochin Airport / Ernakulum railway station and drive to Munnar (04 Hrs) enroute visit Valara and Cheeyappara waterfalls and natural scenic spots. On arrival check in at hotel and relax for rest of the day and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 02 : Munnar

After breakfast start the day with local sightseeing of Munnar Situated at the confluence of three mountain streams - Kundale, Nallathanni & Mudrapuzha and this city is 1600 Meter above sea level. This majestic hill station was once the summer resort of the British government in South India. Sightseeing to visit is Tata Tea Museum (Closed on Monday), Mattupetty Dam, Echo point, Photo Point and in the afternoon proceed to Rajamalai (Eravikulam National Park) were the rarest species of mountain goat, "Nilgiri Thars", can be spotted. Overnight stay at Munnar.

Day 03 : Munnar – Thekkady

Have your delicious breakfast from Munnar check out from hotel and drive to Thekkady (04 Hrs). On arrival check in at hotel later Proceed for the sightseeing tour of Thekkady - The Periyar wildlife sanctuary in Thekkady which is spread across 777 sq. km of which 360 sq. km is thick ever green forest, activities like Boating at Periyar lake, Kathakali, Kalari (Martial Art show), and Spice Plantation can also be done on direct payment basis. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 04 :Day 04 : Thekkady - Alleppey

After your lovely breakfast, check out of the hotel drive to Alleppey (04 Hrs) It's best known for houseboat cruises along the rustic Kerala backwaters Check in at hotel and relax later visit Sree Krishnaswamy temple, light house, Coir Museum & Beach Stay overnight at the hotel.

Day 05 : Alleppey - Cochin

After having your scrumptious breakfast on the final day of your trip, check out from the hotel and drive back to the Cochin Airport / Ernakulam Railway station with a feeling of contented about a wonderful holiday you spent in God's Own Country.
